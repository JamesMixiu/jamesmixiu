Privacy Policy

Personal Data

"Youtube Comment Translator" had never collected and will never collect any sensitive personal data, browsing history etc to a remote server.
"Youtube Comment Translator" may collect anonymous browser version and platform name. This information is needed for decision on implementing new features, removing unused features or suggesting default settings for new users. It will happen only with your permission.

Third Party Services

This extension needs to rely on third-party translation apis, but the translation content is sent anonymously. You can choose your trusted third-party translation api service provider in the settings.
